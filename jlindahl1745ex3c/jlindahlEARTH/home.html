Welcome to EARTH

More than just a planet

Welcome to EARTH. Earth is not just another planet in our solar system, Earth is our home. Earth is where all living beings- great and small- reside. It is the responsibility of all Earthlings to keep our planet healthy and thriving. Thats where we come in. The EARTH website is where we all come together and interact as community of Earthlings.
